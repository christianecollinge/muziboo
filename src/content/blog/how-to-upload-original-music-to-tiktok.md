## The Official Way: Upload Music to TikTok Using a Distributor

If you want to officially release your music, you can use a digital music distributor. This will put your songs in TikTok's commercial music library. These services connect your finished song to many streaming platforms, including TikTok. Think of them as your record label without all the extra work. They handle the details like licenses, song info, and collecting payments. This is the best way to make sure your music is properly ready for use on TikTok. Other creators can then use your sound in their videos. You may also earn money from your music.

When you sign up with a distributor like DistroKid, CD Baby, or TuneCore, you will upload your final song. During this process, you choose which platforms you want your music sent to. TikTok will be one of these choices. After your song is approved, which can take a few days or weeks, it will appear in TikTok's music library. This means any TikTok user can search for your song. They can use it in their videos. This helps your music reach more people. This way is best for finished songs you are ready to share. It helps you build a professional presence on all major music sites.

### Choosing the Right Distributor for Your UK Sound

There are many digital distributors available. Picking the right one can seem hard. For UK artists, think about more than just the price. Look at the services they offer. Do they provide clear reports on how much money you earn? Is their customer support helpful? Do they have good tools to show you where your music is played? Some distributors let you release unlimited songs for a yearly fee. This can be great for artists who make a lot of music. Others might take a small part of your earnings. But they may offer more marketing help or artist support.

Most major distributors work well globally. They make sure your music reaches TikTok users in the UK and worldwide. It is a good idea to read reviews. You can also chat with other independent artists in our Muziboo community. Ask them which services they like best. The best distributor will fit your needs, budget, and music goals. This lets you focus more on making music and less on how to share it.

## The Direct Way: Upload Music to TikTok Yourself

Besides official distribution, there is a faster, more direct way to get your music on TikTok. This is very popular. You simply use your music as the background sound for your own videos. This shows the true spirit of "do it yourself" music. It fits perfectly with Muziboo's goals. You do not need a distributor for this. You just need your music file and the TikTok app. When you make a video on TikTok, you can record sound directly. Or, you can upload a video that already has your music in it.

To do this, make a video using your phone or editing software. Put a short part of your song into the video as the sound. When you upload this video to TikTok, that sound becomes an "original sound." It will be linked to your profile. Other users can then click on your video's sound. They will see it came from you. They can even use that same sound in their own videos. This method is great for trying out new ideas. You can share short bits of music or show how you create. It is perfect for those rough, unfinished pieces you are working on. It is a good way to see what many people think about your music.
